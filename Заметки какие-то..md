Вот правильные ответы с глаголами в Past Simple или Past Continuous:

1. I **went** to the cinema yesterday.  
2. I **was going** to the cinema at four o'clock yesterday.  
3. I **was going** to the cinema when you met me.  
4. I **was doing** my homework the whole evening yesterday.  
5. I **was doing** my homework when mother came home.  
6. I **did** my homework yesterday.  
7. I **was doing** my homework from five till eight yesterday.  
8. I **was doing** my homework at six o'clock yesterday.  
9. I **didn’t play** the piano yesterday. I **wrote** a letter to my friend.  
10. I **wasn’t playing** the piano at four o'clock yesterday. I **was reading** a book.  
11. He **wasn’t sleeping** when father came home. He **was doing** his homework.  

12. When I **was going** to school the day before yesterday, I met Mike and Pete. They **were talking** and **laughing**.  
They told me a funny story. Soon I **laughed**, too. I **was still laughing** when we came to school. After school I **told** this story at home. My father and mother **liked** it very much.  

13. When we were in the country last summer, I **went** to the woods one day. In the woods I **found** a little fox cub. It could not run. It **had injured** its leg. I **decided** to help the cub. I **brought** it home. Every day I **fed** it and **took** care of it. I **was doing** it the whole summer. Now the fox cub is quite well. It **has left** my house. It lives in the woods again.

(Примечание: в предложении 13 для «It (to injure) its leg» чаще используется Past Perfect — **had injured**, так как действие произошло до момента находки. Но в школьных упражнениях иногда допускают Past Simple — **injured**. Вариант с Past Perfect точнее.)
